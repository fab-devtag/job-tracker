About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our core Design Systems team. You will be responsible for defining the visual language and interaction patterns that power Stripe's global products.

Key Responsibilities

  • Evolve the Stripe design system (Sail) to support new product lines.
  • Collaborate with engineering to ensure high-quality implementation.
  • Mentor junior designers and contribute to design culture.
  • Conduct user research to validate component usability.

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in product design.
  • Strong proficiency in Figma and prototyping tools.
  • Experience documenting design patterns.
